Cat Eyes Potion

Description

While it may not be considered an essential potion, cat eye potions are one of the most underratted of the 40 basic potions. With this potioon you will never again have to worry about your torchlight giving away your location at night. No more tripping over roots in the forest. This classic darkvision potion should definitely be on your to-brew list next full moon. One vial of my cat eye potion will allow you to see in the dark as clear as day for a full 6 hours. Its also a great potion recipe to practice putting your own spin on!

Ingredients

  • 4 Small white mushrooms
  • 6 Drops of morning dew
  • 1 Cup of birch bark collected at midnight
  • 2 Tsp of cinamon
  • 2 Frog legs
  • 3 Dust bunnies
  • 2 Live fireflies

Steps

This potion must be prepared outdoors under a full moon.

  1. Finely chop mushrooms and put them in a small mixing bowl.
  2. Add dew and mix gently.
  3. Chop birch bark and add to mushrooms along with cinamon.
  4. Add mixture to an iron cauldron and toast lightly over a fire.
  5. Add 4 cups of water to cauldron and bring to a boil, stirring occasionally.
  6. Once boiling add frog legs whole. Boil until legs turn soft.
  7. One at a time, add dust bunnies to the cauldron, allowing each to dissolve fully.
  8. With large metal tweezers grab fireflies and hold them in the boiling potion until they break apart.
  9. With a cheesecloth, strain all chunks out of the potion.
  10. Pour into 8 small glass bottles and store them in a dark location.
