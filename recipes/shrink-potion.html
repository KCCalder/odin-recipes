Shrink Potion

Description

Ever have a pesky customer that you wish you could just keep in a jar? Or maybe you need to sneak through a tiny crack in a wall? Every respectable witch needs a go to shrink potion recipe. This recipe has been passed through my family for generations of witches and works dependably regardless of how long its been stored. Drink one bottle of it and get shrunk down to 5% of your original height within minutes, with effects lasting up to 4 hours.

Ingredients

  • 2 Cups dry river mud
  • 2 Drops of salamander tears
  • 6 Pigeon feathers (shed not plucked)
  • 1/4 Cup crushed buttercup petals
  • 1 Eye of newt
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Bring 8 cups of water to a boil in a medium copper cauldron over an open flame.
  2. Gradually sift in the dry river mud while stirring the mixture.
  3. Add two drops of salamander tears then leave to boil for 10 minutes.
  4. Finely chop the pidgeon feathers and add to a seperate bowl with the buttercup petals. Mix until a faint cooing can be heard.
  5. Throw the eye of newt into the fire heating the cauldron.
  6. While the fire is green, quickly add the petal and feather mixture to the cauldron.
  7. Stir gently until the potion turns a sparkling blue.
  8. Add salt to taste.
  9. Pour finished potion into 250mL glass bottles and store them in a cool, dry location.
